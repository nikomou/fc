# Flex Commerce — Competitor Analysis

**Last updated:** March 2026

---

## 1. Competitor Overview

The UK Shopify Plus agency market has a clear tier structure:

| Tier | Agencies | Characteristics |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 (National, well-established) | Swanky Agency, Velstar, Eastside Co | High DR (50+), large content output, multiple Shopify Plus certifications |
| Tier 2 (Growing national) | Underwaterpistol, Statement Agency, Noughts & Ones | DR 30–50, strong case studies, regional offices |
| Tier 3 (Regional specialists) | Flex Commerce, We Make Websites (acquired), We Are Saboteur | DR 10–30, strong local presence, growing national reach |

Flex Commerce currently sits at the top of Tier 3 and should target Tier 2 positioning within 18 months.

## 2. Individual Competitor Profiles

### 2.1 Swanky Agency

**Website:** swankyagency.com
**Estimated DR:** 52
**Estimated organic traffic:** 12,000–18,000/month
**Locations:** Exeter (HQ), London, New York

**SEO Strengths:**
- Exceptional case study library (30+ detailed studies with revenue metrics)
- Extensive blog covering Shopify Plus, headless commerce, and DTC strategy
- Strong backlink profile from Shopify official partner listings and international ecommerce press
- Ranks well for "headless Shopify" and "Shopify Plus fashion agency" — premium niche positioning
- Well-structured service pages (2,000+ words each) with internal FAQ sections

**SEO Weaknesses:**
- Thin local/location SEO — no UK city-specific pages
- Limited migration content — opportunity for Flex Commerce
- No pricing transparency — Flex has an advantage here
- Slow to update content (some pages last updated 2023)

**Keyword gaps Flex Commerce can exploit:**
- All UK city-specific Shopify agency queries
- Platform migration comparison content (Magento vs Shopify, WooCommerce vs Shopify)
- Pricing/cost content ("Shopify Plus agency cost UK")

### 2.2 Velstar

**Website:** velstar.co.uk
**Estimated DR:** 45
**Estimated organic traffic:** 8,000–12,000/month
**Locations:** Liverpool (HQ), Manchester, London

**SEO Strengths:**
- Excellent location page strategy — Liverpool, Manchester, London pages rank well
- Strong content covering Shopify Plus migrations
- Active PR presence in UK ecommerce press
- Clean site structure with clear service taxonomy
- Good use of review schema and Clutch badge

**SEO Weaknesses:**
- Limited guides/how-to content depth — mostly short articles
- Thin app development and integrations content
- No checklist or template content
- Limited B2B/wholesale Shopify content

**Competitive threat level for Flex Commerce: HIGH**
Velstar is a direct competitor sharing Liverpool and Manchester markets. Flex Commerce must differentiate on content depth, case study quality, and response speed.

**Gap opportunities:**
- `/guides/` depth (Velstar has ~20 guides; Flex has 60+ planned)
- Shopify AI and automation content — Velstar has nothing in this space
- B2B/wholesale Shopify content cluster
- More granular service sub-pages (e.g., Shopify Liquid development, Headless Shopify)

### 4.2 Industry Vertical Pages (Missing — High Priority)

None of the competitors fully own industry verticals in UK Shopify SEO. This is a significant white space opportunity:

**Recommended industry pages to build:**
- `/shopify-for-fashion` — fashion & apparel (Manchester strength)
- `/shopify-for-health-beauty` — health & beauty DTC
- `/shopify-for-food-drink` — food & beverage ecommerce
- `/shopify-for-b2b` — B2B/wholesale Shopify Plus
- `/shopify-for-luxury` — premium/luxury brands
- `/shopify-for-sports-fitness` — sports & fitness (Gymshark angle)
- `/shopify-for-home-garden` — home, interiors, garden

Each page should be 1,500+ words targeting "[industry] shopify agency" and "[industry] ecommerce agency uk" queries.

### 4.3 Partner Integration Pages (Missing — Medium Priority)

Flex Commerce lists 10 technology partners (Yotpo, Nosto, Klaviyo, Mollie, Reviews.io, Trustpilot, Gorgias, Klevu, Brightpearl, Patchworks) but has no dedicated integration pages. Competitors have none either — first-mover advantage available.
