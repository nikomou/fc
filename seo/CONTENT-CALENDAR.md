## Content Quality Standards

### Every Article Must Include
- Target keyword in: title, H1, first 100 words, meta description, at least 2 H2s, image alt text
- Word count: minimum 1,200 words; flagship articles 2,500+
- Named author with link to team profile
- Internal links: minimum 3 to service/location pages, 2 to related content
- CTA section: link to relevant service page + quote CTA
- Schema: Article/BlogPosting with author, datePublished, dateModified
- OpenGraph image: custom per article (not generic)
- Estimated read time in article header

### Every Guide Must Include
- Step-by-step numbered structure
- Code examples or screenshots where applicable
- HowTo schema with steps
- Link to relevant service page ("Need help with this? Talk to our team")
- Minimum 1,500 words

### Every Checklist Must Include
- Scannable numbered/checkmark list
- Grouped by category
- Downloadable PDF version (optional but valuable for links)
- ItemList schema
- CTA at end

### Case Studies Must Include
- Client name or industry + city
- Challenge statement
- Approach and tools used
- Specific measurable results (%, £ values, time savings)
- Client testimonial quote
- Timeline (start to completion)
- Services delivered (with links)
- Before/after screenshots where possible
