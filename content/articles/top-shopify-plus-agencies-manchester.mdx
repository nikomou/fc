## How to Choose the Right Manchester Shopify Plus Agency

Partner tier matters, but it's not the only factor. Platinum and Premier Partners have hit higher revenue and client volume thresholds — but a boutique Plus Partner with relevant sector experience can outperform a larger agency that doesn't know your market. Ask to see work for brands similar to yours in size and category, and always meet the team who will actually work on your account.

- Check partner tier: Plus, Platinum, or Premier
- Ask for case studies in your product category and revenue range
- Understand their retainer vs project model — ongoing support is often where the value is
- Meet the team working on your account, not just the pitch team
