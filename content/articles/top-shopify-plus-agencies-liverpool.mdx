Liverpool has a growing reputation as a serious ecommerce city, with a cluster of Shopify Plus agencies serving everything from independent DTC brands to global enterprise retailers. The city's proximity to Manchester means you get access to the same deep Shopify talent pool — with agencies that have built impressive client rosters across fashion, sport, and consumer goods.

## Top Shopify Plus Agencies in Liverpool

They specialise in bespoke Shopify Plus builds, AI-powered automations, custom integrations, and intelligent reporting dashboards — bringing enterprise-level capability to brands at every stage of growth.

- Shopify Plus Partner with a Liverpool office
- Specialises in AI agents, automation, and custom Shopify development
- Works with D2C and B2B brands on complex Shopify Plus projects
- Dual-city presence: Manchester and Liverpool

### 2. Velstar

<ArticleImage src="/images/blog/agencies/velstar.jpg" alt="Velstar homepage — Shopify Platinum Partner Liverpool" caption="velstar.agency" />

Velstar is Liverpool's flagship Shopify Plus agency — a Shopify Platinum Partner (the highest tier) with additional hubs in Leeds, Edinburgh, and Miami. They operate as a full-service D2C and B2B ecommerce agency covering strategy, design, engineering, and growth. Their client list is among the most impressive of any UK Shopify agency.

- Shopify Platinum Partner — highest tier available
- Clients include Nando's, French Connection, McLaren, AVON, and Tangle Teezer
- Delivered four-region multi-store Shopify Plus setup for French Connection
- Migrated AVON's global ecommerce to Shopify Plus

Epic Web Service is a Shopify agency serving Liverpool businesses with new store builds, redesigns, replatforming to Shopify Plus, and ongoing support. A solid option for Liverpool SMEs looking for a local specialist with hands-on Shopify capability.

## How to Choose a Shopify Plus Agency in Liverpool

Liverpool's ecommerce agency market is smaller than Manchester's, but the quality at the top end is exceptional. Velstar operates at true enterprise scale, while Flex Commerce covers the mid-market with deep technical and strategic capability. For complex builds, migrations, and bespoke integrations, prioritise agencies with Plus or Platinum partner status and relevant sector experience.
